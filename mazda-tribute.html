Powerful, yet economical. The 2010 Mazda Tribute gives you the choice of 4-cylinder or V6 engines, excellent fuel economy - up to 28 mpg†, rugged good looks and an outstanding 5-Star crash-safety rating‡. It's the Small SUV smart choice for 2010.

Price: $45,800

Engine: 2.5-liter DOHC 16-valve 4-cylinder with VVT

Transmission: Manual & Automatic

Braking System: Front disc/rear drum brakes

Fuel system: Electronically controlled multiport fuel injection

Steering type: Power rack-and-pinion

Available Colors: Flaxen Mica, Medium Silver, Black, White, Strong Blue, Gun Metallic, Silver

Audio: AM/FM/CD stereo with 4 speakers

Safety: Anti-lock Brake System (ABS) w/ Electronic Brakeforce Distribution (EBD)

Tire and Wheel: P235/70R16 all-season radial tires

Lights: Halogen headlights and Halogen fog lights
